FitCircle — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

About FitCircle

FitCircle is an invite-only social fitness accountability app that helps users stay consistent with their gym and fitness routines through squad-based accountability and streaks.

Information We Collect

  • Email address and name (for account creation)
  • Gym location (to connect you with local members)
  • Check-in data (dates and times you visit the gym)
  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weight)
  • Posts and videos you share on the feed
  • Device token (for push notifications)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the FitCircle service
  • To calculate and display your streak and squad stats
  • To send morning motivation push notifications
  • To show your posts to other members at your gym location

Third Party Services

FitCircle uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, file storage, and push notifications. Google Privacy Policy

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile tab within the app, which permanently removes all your data from our systems.

Account Deletion

To delete your account: open FitCircle → Profile tab → Delete Account. This permanently deletes your account, all posts, check-ins, and workout data.

PIPEDA Compliance

FitCircle complies with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You have the right to access, correct, and request deletion of your personal information.

Children's Privacy

FitCircle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Common Issues

  • Can't register — You need a valid invite code from an existing member
  • Check-in not working — Make sure you're connected to your gym's WiFi or use the manual check-in button
  • Forgot password — Use the "Forgot password?" link on the login screen
  • Delete account — Profile tab → Delete Account
